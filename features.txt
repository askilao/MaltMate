Whiskey app that lets you look up whiskeys
see where they are distilled and scan barcodes to find whiskey.
Lets you add favorites and log a review of the whiskey.

MAIN MENU:
	Add whiskey:
		List of whiskeys:
			Details option:
				Shows info about whiskey
				including where distillery is on map
			Compare two whiskeys
		Select a whiskey from list:
			Add rating 0-10
			Add note
			Optional: add to favorite
		Find whiskey from barcode:
			Open barcode activity:
			scan barcode
			Add ratin 0-10
			Add note
			Optional: add to favorite
	Show favorites:
		Shows whiskeys added to favorites
		Details option
		Rating
		Note
